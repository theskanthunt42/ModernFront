## Case first, then *case studies*

I used to mentioned on my Telegram Channel (I guess) about something I observed in a *cloudy* summer day.

I know you guys will just call it as [Conformity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conformity) *et cetera\[sic\]* or whatever, but when I check online about it it's more like [Collective delusion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pluralistic_ignorance) when (at least in my POV, I am no expert on this) many people happen to in a single place and they are mostly alone not in a group with their friends or sth in that place and suddenly someone in this many people decides to do or believe on something wrong or stupid and others just follow suit without their own judgement.

So tldr:

- It have been raining for a few days, and that day was quite cloudy. (just like days before it)
- Some waterponds formed due to reason above.
- And because of the cloudy weather most of the students were taking umbrella with them, incl. me.
- But it never rained that day.
- It happened when the first class in the afternoon just end and most were leaving the college building via the front entrance.
- I was leaving too but the entrance were so crowdy and I am quite confused with it, so I started to observing others.

So the first thing I did when I saw some are opening the umbrella is looking at the sky but found out it wasn't raining at all, not even windy(You could tell by leaves weren't moving at all, either by rain drops or by wind), but:  

Someone walked out of the building, and stopped to looking at the skies, and are opening their umbrellas.  
Others noticing this did the same as well.  

And the end result was most of them just stopped and did the same and now theres a little traffic jam going on.  
And btw I heard someone talking to their pals said: "Hey it's raining just put on your umbrella!"
